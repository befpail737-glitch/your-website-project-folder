🏭 EV Charging Infrastructure

Reducing EV Charging Station Costs by 20%

The Challenge

A leading European EV charging station manufacturer needed a reliable, cost-effective power solution for their next-generation AC charging piles. Their existing supplier had long lead times (8-12 weeks) and high costs, impacting their ability to scale production.

The Solution

LiTong's engineering team recommended MORNSUN's LM series AC/DC converters (LM75-20B24) for the auxiliary power supply and TD501D485H-E isolated transceivers for CAN communication. The compact size and high efficiency of MORNSUN modules allowed for a more compact charging station design.

Products Used

  • LM75-20B24 - AC/DC converter for auxiliary power (75W, 24V output)
  • TD501D485H-E - Isolated CAN transceiver for communication
  • B0505S-1WR3 - DC/DC converter for isolation

🏭 Industrial Automation

24/7 Reliable Power for Smart Factory

The Challenge

A major automotive parts manufacturer in the USA needed a power supply solution for their new smart factory line. The system required 24/7 operation with zero downtime tolerance, and the power supplies needed to withstand harsh industrial environments with high vibration and temperature variations.

The Solution

LiTong recommended MORNSUN's LH series high-reliability AC/DC converters and URB series wide-input DC/DC converters. The -40°C to +85°C operating temperature range and 5G vibration resistance made them ideal for the factory floor.

  • LH60-10B24 - High-reliability AC/DC converter (60W, DIN-rail)

🏥 Medical Equipment

Medical-Grade Isolation for Patient Monitoring

The Challenge

A medical device startup developing a new patient monitoring system needed a power supply solution that met strict medical safety standards (2xMOPP isolation, low leakage current <100µA) while maintaining a compact form factor for portable use.

The Solution

LiTong's FAE team recommended MORNSUN's MPM series medical-grade AC/DC converters with 2xMOPP isolation and <75µA leakage current. The compact 2"x3" package fit perfectly in their portable design.

Products Used

  • MPM-30S24 - Medical-grade AC/DC converter (30W, 2xMOPP)
  • B1212S-1WR3 - Isolated DC/DC for signal isolation
  • TD501MCANFD - Isolated CAN FD transceiver
