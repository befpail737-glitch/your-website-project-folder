Strategic Expansion Initiative

Investing in infrastructure to support global growth

LiTong Power, a leading authorized distributor of MORNSUN power conversion solutions, today announced a significant expansion of its global distribution network. The comprehensive initiative includes new warehouse facilities, enhanced logistics capabilities, and expanded regional support centers designed to better serve customers worldwide.

This strategic investment reinforces LiTong's commitment to providing exceptional customer service while supporting the growing global demand for high-quality power electronics solutions. The expansion will reduce delivery times, increase inventory availability, and enhance technical support capabilities across key markets.

Customer Benefits

Direct improvements to service experience

Reduced Lead Times

The expanded network significantly reduces shipping distances and transit times. Customers in major metropolitan areas can now expect next-day delivery for in-stock items, while remote locations will see delivery times reduced by 2-3 days on average.

Enhanced Technical Support

Each regional hub includes dedicated Field Application Engineers (FAEs) providing local language support and timezone-appropriate service. This ensures customers receive expert technical assistance when and where they need it most.
