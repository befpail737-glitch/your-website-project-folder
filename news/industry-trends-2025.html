Emerging Technologies

Gallium Nitride (GaN) Revolution

GaN technology continues to gain momentum, offering superior efficiency and power density compared to traditional silicon solutions. In 2025, we're seeing:

  • Cost reduction making GaN more accessible for mainstream applications
  • Improved thermal management solutions enabling higher power densities
  • Integration with intelligent control systems for optimal performance

AI-Driven Power Design

Artificial intelligence is revolutionizing power supply design and optimization:

  • Automated component selection and circuit optimization
  • Predictive maintenance and fault detection
  • Dynamic load management and efficiency optimization

Application-Specific Trends

Electric Vehicle Charging Infrastructure

The EV charging market is experiencing explosive growth, driving demand for:

  • High-power DC fast charging systems (350kW+)
  • Bidirectional power flow capabilities (V2G/V2H)
  • Smart grid integration and load balancing

Data Center Power Solutions

Hyperscale data centers are pushing the boundaries of power efficiency:

  • 48V power architectures becoming mainstream
  • Liquid cooling integration with power delivery
  • AI-optimized power management systems
