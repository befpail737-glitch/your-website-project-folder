General Questions

Where can I buy authentic MORNSUN products?

You can buy authentic MORNSUN products from authorized distributors like LiTong Power. As a Tier 1 authorized distributor, we guarantee 100% genuine MORNSUN power modules with full traceability and manufacturer warranty. Avoid unauthorized sellers to prevent counterfeit products.

How do I verify if a distributor is authorized by MORNSUN?

To verify an authorized MORNSUN distributor, check for: 1) Official authorization certificate from MORNSUN, 2) Listed on MORNSUN's official website distributor page, 3) Ability to provide original manufacturer warranty, 4) Direct technical support from MORNSUN FAE team. LiTong Power is a verified Tier 1 distributor with official authorization.

Technical Support

Do you offer technical support for MORNSUN power modules?

Yes, LiTong Power provides comprehensive technical support including: product selection guidance, application circuit design, EMC/EMI recommendations, thermal management advice, and troubleshooting assistance. How do I choose the right AC/DC or DC/DC converter for my application?

Consider these factors: input voltage range, output voltage/current requirements, isolation needs, efficiency requirements, operating temperature, package size, and safety certifications. Our FAE team can provide free selection guidance - contact us with your specifications for recommendations.

Are MORNSUN products RoHS and CE compliant?

Yes, all MORNSUN products are RoHS compliant (2011/65/EU) and carry CE marking for European markets. Many products also have UL, cUL, and CB certifications. We can provide certificates of compliance and full technical documentation upon request.
