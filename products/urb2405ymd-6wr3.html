Solution: EV Charging Station Auxiliary Power

● Solution Overview

The reliability of an Electric Vehicle (EV) charging station is critically dependent on its auxiliary power system. This system powers the main controller, human-machine interface (HMI), communication modules, and safety relays. Our solution, built around MORNSUN's specialized power modules, addresses the key challenges of this application: wide input voltage range, harsh outdoor temperature fluctuations, high electromagnetic interference, and the need for long-term reliability.

● System Block Diagram

● Core Advantages

  • High Reliability: Utilizes industrial-grade components with a wide operating temperature range (-40℃ to +85℃).
  • Superior EMC Performance: Modules are designed to meet CISPR32/EN55032 Class B standards, ensuring stable operation in noisy electrical environments.
  • Compact & Integrated: Highly integrated modules save valuable PCB space and simplify the design process.
  • Safety Compliance: Products meet key safety standards, ensuring safe operation for both the equipment and the end-user.
