Frequently Asked Questions

What does 2xMOPP mean for medical applications?

2xMOPP (Means of Patient Protection) signifies the highest level of electrical safety and insulation required for medical devices that may come into contact with patients. It ensures protection against electric shock even if one layer of insulation fails.

What is the difference between convection and forced-air power ratings?

The convection rating (300W) is the maximum power the supply can deliver with only natural air circulation. The forced-air rating (450W) is the maximum power it can deliver when a fan provides a specified airflow (CFM) across the unit to cool it.
