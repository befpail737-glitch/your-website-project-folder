MORNSUN LM75-20B24

75W Enclosed AC/DC Switching Power Supply with Universal Input and 24V Output

FAE Distributor's Review

The LM75-20B24 is a workhorse product we frequently recommend to our industrial control clients. Its standout features are its robust EMC performance (meeting CISPR32/EN55032 CLASS B without external components) and its wide operating temperature range of -30℃ to +70℃. This significantly simplifies system design and enhances reliability in harsh environments. Compared to similar models, its high efficiency and low no-load power consumption make it an excellent choice for energy-conscious applications.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does this model require external filtering for EMC compliance?

No, the LM75-20B24 is designed to meet CISPR32/EN55032 CLASS B standards for both conducted and radiated emissions internally, simplifying your design and saving board space.

What is the maximum capacitive load this power supply can start up?

Please refer to the official datasheet for the detailed capacitive load curve. However, it is robustly designed to handle typical loads found in industrial automation systems.

Can the output voltage be adjusted?

Yes, the output voltage can be slightly adjusted via the "ADJ" potentiometer. The typical adjustment range is around ±10%.
