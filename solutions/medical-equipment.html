Solution: Industrial Control & Automation

● Solution Overview

Powering the next generation of industrial automation requires power supplies that can withstand harsh electrical environments, including high noise, voltage fluctuations, and extreme temperatures. This solution showcases a complete power architecture for Programmable Logic Controllers (PLCs), robotics, and distributed control systems. It emphasizes high reliability, superior EMC performance, and signal integrity to ensure maximum uptime and precision.

● System Block Diagram

● Core Advantages

  • Robust EMC Performance: Products selected meet stringent industrial standards (CISPR32/EN55032 Class B), minimizing interference with sensitive analog and digital circuits.
  • High Isolation: High-isolation DC/DC converters protect control circuits from high voltage transients and break ground loops between different system parts.
  • Wide Operating Temperature: Components are rated for -40℃ to +85℃, ensuring reliable operation in unconditioned factory environments.
  • DIN-Rail Compatibility: Standardized mounting options for easy and fast installation in control cabinets.
