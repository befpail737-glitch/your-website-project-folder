Solution: Medical Equipment Power

● Solution Overview

Safety and reliability are non-negotiable in medical device design. Power supplies for medical equipment must meet stringent standards for patient and operator protection, specifically IEC/EN60601-1. This solution focuses on using MORNSUN's medically certified AC/DC power supplies, which feature reinforced insulation (2xMOPP) and ultra-low leakage currents to ensure the highest levels of safety for devices like patient monitoring systems, medical imaging machines, and diagnostic equipment.
